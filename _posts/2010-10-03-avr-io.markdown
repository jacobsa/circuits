How to hook up the cheap [AVR-ISP500 programmer] [sparkfun] I bought on
Sparkfun (programmer/header pin to AVR pin):

 *  `V_TAR` to `Vcc`
 *  `MOSI` to `MOSI`
 *  `GND` to `GND`
 *  `MISO` to `MISO`
 *  `SCK` to `SCK`
 *  `TRST` to `RESET`

[sparkfun]: http://www.sparkfun.com/commerce/product_info.php?products_id=8702
